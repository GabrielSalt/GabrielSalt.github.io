About this piece ⓘ

We produced a live computer-generated installation that projects unique and ever-changing silhouettes resembling a swarm.
This dynamic artwork is not only visually captivating but also incorporates motion capture technology and is audio-reactive, creating an immersive and interactive experience for the audience.
The constantly evolving visuals respond to the movements of viewers and ambient sounds, making each presentation of the work a singular experience.


Produced on Touchdesigner, presented for the IC Hackspace 2024 - Winning People's Choice Award
